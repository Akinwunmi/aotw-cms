Welcome to Flag Archive, a tapestry of all flags from around the globe!

The goal of this project is to showcase every flag in the world, offering a unique and interactive experience for all who are passionate about flags, geopolitics, and symbols.

The Background

Flag Archive was founded by Jurrit van der Ploeg, a dedicated developer and enthusiastic amateur vexillologist. Jurrit his approach was to create a platform where people could delve into the huge landscape of flags, understanding their historical contexts and geographical significance. What began as a personal passion project is gradually growing into a dynamic and expansive digital archive.

The Approach

Flag Archive offers a multi-layered exploration of flags:

Geographical Navigation
Explore flags by country, region, and continent. See how flags represent diverse cultures and national identities around the world.
Historical Navigation
Travel through time to see how flags have evolved. Witness the changing landscape of flags as nations rise, fall, and transform.
Interactive Experience
The platform allows you to navigate through different eras and locations seamlessly, providing an engaging way to learn about the symbolism and history behind each flag.

The Features

Account Creation
Sign up to create an account and keep track of your favorite flags. There are functionalities to come in the future.
Continuous Updates
We strive to keep our database current, adding new flags and updating information as it becomes available.
